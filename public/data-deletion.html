Data Deletion — MARTIVI ADS MANAGER

Last updated: 2026-05-16

Martivi Digital LLC ("we") respects your right to control your data. This page explains how to request deletion of any data the MARTIVI ADS MANAGER Facebook application (App ID 1131335258922007) has accessed or stored about you, your Facebook account, or any Meta business asset you authorized.

Two ways to request deletion

Option 1 — Through Facebook (recommended)

  1. Open https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications
  2. Find MARTIVI ADS MANAGER in your installed apps
  3. Click Remove

Facebook will send us a deletion request automatically. We act on the request within 30 days.

Option 2 — Email us directly

Send an email to privacy@martividigital.com with:

  • Subject: Data deletion request — MARTIVI ADS MANAGER
  • The Facebook account, Page, Business Manager, or Ad Account identifier you want deleted
  • A brief description of what you'd like deleted (all data, specific assets, etc.)

We will: - Acknowledge your request within 3 business days - Complete deletion within 30 days - Send a confirmation email when complete

If we cannot fulfill the request (for example, where retention is required to comply with law), we will tell you why and what we can do instead.

What gets deleted

We delete (or de-identify) all Platform Data we hold that relates to your request, including:

  • OAuth access tokens and System User credentials linked to your account or Business
  • Cached ad account, campaign, ad set, ad, and insight data
  • Cached Page content and metadata
  • Cached catalog data
  • Ad creatives we generated or stored on your behalf
  • Operational logs containing your identifiers

We may retain:

  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics that cannot be linked to you
  • Records of the deletion request itself, for 24 months, to evidence compliance
  • Data we are legally required to retain (e.g., for tax, audit, or regulatory purposes)

Confirmation

If you submitted via Facebook (Option 1), Facebook itself confirms back to you that we processed the request. We also accept a confirmation_code at this endpoint:

Confirmation endpoint: https://martiviconsulting.com/api/data-deletion/status?id={confirmation_code}

This endpoint returns a JSON response describing the status of your deletion request. You will receive the confirmation_code either by email (Option 2) or as part of Facebook's response (Option 1).
