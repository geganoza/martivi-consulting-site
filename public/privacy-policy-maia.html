3. Data we access from Meta

We expressly enumerate the categories of Platform Data we may access:

Identity & authentication - Facebook User ID, name, email (when available), profile picture - System User identifiers and OAuth access tokens

Business assets - Ad Account IDs, Business Manager IDs, Page IDs, Catalog IDs, Pixel/Dataset IDs, and metadata associated with each

Advertising data - Campaigns, ad sets, ads, ad creatives (images, video, copy), targeting parameters, budgets, bids, schedules - Performance metrics: impressions, reach, clicks, click-through rate, cost-per-click, spend, frequency, conversions, return on ad spend, post-engagement metrics

Page content - Page posts, scheduled posts, photos, videos, captions, comments and reactions on Page posts, Page messages metadata - We do not access the body of private inbound messages unless a future Messaging permission is added

Catalogs & commerce - Product feed data, SKUs, prices, availability, product images, product sets

Insights and webhooks - Page insights, audience demographics, content performance, Page-level events

Technical / operational - IP address, browser/device user-agent, log timestamps for our internal request logs

We do not access: end-user payment details, identity documents, biometric data, location beyond Page-level region, health data, or message bodies.

7. AI / machine-learning disclosure

Platform Data is never used to train any machine-learning or generative model, ours or any third party's. When we send drafts or briefs to AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI) we do so under terms that prohibit training, retention beyond response generation, and any human review of inputs.

8. Data retention

Category Retention
Active campaign data While the campaign or ad set is active
Archived / paused campaign data and insights 90 days after archive, then aggregated only
Ad creative assets (images, video) Until the client requests deletion or for 90 days after final use
Page post content the App created While the post is live on the Page; deletion requests honored on receipt

Operational logs 90 days

Deletion-request records 24 months, to evidence compliance

We do not retain Platform Data indefinitely. Requests under §11 are completed within 30 days.

9. Security measures

  • Transport: TLS 1.2 or higher for all data in transit
  • Storage: AES-256 encryption at rest for credentials and tokens; database-level encryption where supported by our provider
  • Access control: role-based access; OAuth tokens accessible only to the System User they belong to
  • Auditing: API call logs reviewed monthly
  • Incident response: notification to affected users and to Meta within 72 hours of confirmation of a Platform Data breach, per Platform Terms §6

Where the EU/UK GDPR applies, transfers outside the EEA/UK rely on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the UK International Data Transfer Addendum, supplemented by additional safeguards.

11. Your rights and how to exercise them

You — or the business you authorized — have the following rights regarding Platform Data we hold about you or your business assets:

Under GDPR (EU / UK / EEA): - Right of access (Art. 15) - Right to rectification (Art. 16) - Right to erasure / "to be forgotten" (Art. 17) - Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18) - Right to data portability (Art. 20) - Right to object (Art. 21) - Right to withdraw consent at any time - Right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

Under CCPA/CPRA (California residents): - Right to know what is collected and to whom it is disclosed - Right to delete - Right to correct - Right to opt out of sale or sharing — we do not sell or share - Right to non-discrimination for exercising any of the above

To make a request: email privacy@martividigital.com or submit the data deletion form at https://martiviconsulting.com/data-deletion.html. Include the Facebook account, Page, or Business Manager identifier the request relates to.

We respond within 30 days. If we cannot, we will tell you why and how long we need.

12. Data deletion specifically

Two paths exist:

(a) Self-service via Meta. Remove the App from your Facebook account at Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites → MARTIVI ADS MANAGER → Remove. Meta will send us a deletion request automatically.

(b) Direct request. Email privacy@martividigital.com or use the form at https://martiviconsulting.com/data-deletion.html.

Upon request or upon Meta's notification, we delete (or de-identify) all Platform Data within 30 days. Operational logs and aggregated, fully de-identified analytics may be retained.

The App's Data Deletion Request Callback URL is published in the App Dashboard.
